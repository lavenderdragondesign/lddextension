Privacy Policy

LDD MD Enhancer collects no user data. Your prompts, settings, and preferences stay on your device — always. Here's exactly how that works.

📅 Last updated: March 2026
🔒

The short version: we collect nothing.

LDD MD Enhancer has no servers, no analytics, no accounts, and no tracking of any kind. Everything — your saved prompts, theme settings, and tool preferences — is stored locally in your own browser using chrome.storage.local and never leaves your device. That's it.

📦
What data does the extension collect? ▼

None. LDD MD Enhancer collects absolutely no user data. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, account credentials)
  • Browsing history or page content
  • Keystrokes or form inputs
  • Usage analytics or event tracking
  • Device identifiers or IP addresses
  • Crash reports or telemetry
💾
What is stored and where? ▼

The extension stores the following data locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local:

  • Your saved prompt clipboard items (names and content)
  • Theme preferences (colors, brightness, custom presets)
  • Tool toggle settings (Quick Actions Bar, Drag & Drop, etc.)
  • QA bar button visibility preferences

This data is stored only on your device. It is never transmitted to any server, never synced to a cloud service, and is not accessible to anyone other than you. If you uninstall the extension, this data is deleted by the browser.

🌐
Does the extension make any network requests? ▼

No external network requests are made by the extension itself. It does not communicate with any API, server, or third-party service.

The extension operates exclusively on mydesigns.io — this is its only declared host permission. It does not access any other website or domain.

🍪
Cookies and tracking ▼

The extension does not use cookies, tracking pixels, fingerprinting techniques, or any form of cross-site tracking. No analytics libraries are loaded or executed.

🔑
Permissions explained ▼

The extension requests the following browser permissions. Here's exactly why each is needed:

  • storage — to save your prompts, settings, and preferences locally in your browser
  • tabs — to check whether the currently active tab is on mydesigns.io before enabling the extension
  • windows — to open the PDF Generator and Resizer tools in a focused popup window
  • host_permissions: https://mydesigns.io/* — to inject the toolbar, panel, and tools onto the MyDesigns listings page only

No broad host permissions (like <all_urls>) are requested. The extension only activates on one specific site.

👶
Children's privacy ▼

Since we collect no data whatsoever from any user, this extension inherently complies with children's privacy regulations including COPPA. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone of any age.
