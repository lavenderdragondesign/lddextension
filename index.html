Get started

Install in under a minute.

No account, no setup. Until the Chrome Web Store listing is live, install directly from GitHub.

1

Download the extension

Click the download button above or download directly here. Extract the ZIP somewhere permanent — don't delete the folder afterwards.

2

Open Extensions

In Chrome, Edge, Brave, or Opera go to Manage Extensions and enable Developer Mode (top right toggle).

3

Load unpacked

Click Load Unpacked (top left) and select the extracted extension folder.

4a

Pin it

Click the 🧩 puzzle icon in your browser toolbar, find LDD MD Enhancer, and hit the 📌 pin icon.

5

Open MD and go

Navigate to mydesigns.io/app/listings and look for the LDD logo in the bottom right corner. Click it to open the enhancer.
