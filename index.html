Install Steps

Step 1

Download and extract the extension folder

⚠️ Don't delete this folder later

Step 2

In your browser, go to Manage Extensions

Step 3

Toggle Developer Mode (top‑right). Then click Load Unpacked (top‑left) and select the extracted folder

Step 4a

Click the puzzle icon to find and pin the extension

Step 4b

Click the puzzle icon again, find the extension, open the menu (three dots), click Manage Extension and enable Allow User Scripts

Step 5

Open MD on the Dashboard / Listings page and look for the LavenderDragonDesign Logo in the bottom right corner and click it to start

Need Help?

⚠️ If the LavenderDragonDesign logo is not showing at the bottom right in MD,
click the extension icon in your browser toolbar, hit Disable then re-enable it.
