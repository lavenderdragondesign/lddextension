The Dock

Dock-first.
Not sidebar
first.

The dock lives at the bottom of every MyDesigns.io page. It's hidden until you need it, wakes instantly, and keeps every tool exactly one click away — no menus, no hunting.

🧩

Modular controls

Toggle the MD Enhancer, Dock, Drag & Drop, and Auto Rename independently. Use what you need, nothing else.

⚡

Context-aware layout

The dock adapts to where you are — listing view, Vision AI, Dream AI, or the canvas. Different page, right tools.

🎨

Auto-hide with smart wake

Auto-hides after your set delay. Snaps back instantly when you hover a wake zone. Never in the way.

⚙️

Dock Settings — full control

Customise auto-hide delay, dock theme, chevron effect, and toggle the Buy Me a Coffee button on or off — all from the dock's own settings panel.

Wake Hotspots

Three ways to
wake the dock

Never click a button just to open your toolbar again. Hover any wake zone and the dock glides up instantly.

01

Bottom-Left Corner

Hover the bottom-left edge of any MyDesigns.io page. The dock rises without you lifting your eyes from the content area.

02

Bottom-Right Corner

Same trigger on the right side. Works wherever your cursor naturally rests at the end of a row of listing cards.

03

MD Enhancer Bubble

The LDD bubble that sits inline on the page is also a wake point. Hover it and the dock appears — no click required.

Vision AI Profiles

Set it once.
Load it
forever.

Pull & Paste Dream Prompt

Dream prompt
on demand.

Build a prompt in Dream AI, pull it into your library with one click, and paste it right back — any time, any session.

1

Open Dream AI & craft your prompt

Write your image generation prompt directly in Dream AI's interface — tweak it until it's exactly right.

dream.ai/dreamer
2

Pull it into Prompt Manager

Hit Pull Current Dream Prompt in the Prompt Manager. The extension reads the active prompt and saves it as a named entry.

auto-named · stored locally
3

Paste it anywhere, anytime

Find it in your Dream Prompts list and hit Paste to Dream AI. Works across sessions — your prompts don't disappear.

cross-session · one click
Saved Custom Instructions

Stop
rebuilding
prompts.

Write your Custom Instructions once, save them by name, and paste them directly into Vision AI's CI field with a single click — no copying, no tabs.

💾

Save by listing type

Keep a separate saved CI for mugs, tees, posters, hoodies — whatever product types you run. Name them anything.

⚡

Direct paste to Vision AI's CI field

The extension targets the exact Custom Instructions textarea inside Vision AI. It pastes programmatically — no clipboard juggling.

🔄

Works with Vision AI Profiles

Vision AI Profiles also capture your CI text. Paste a CI separately, or load a full profile that includes it — both paths work.
